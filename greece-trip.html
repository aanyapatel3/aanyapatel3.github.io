🧭At a Glance

Relaxed & romantic · beaches, swimming & boat days · no rental car needed anywhere.

✈️ Arrive Jul 6 → Naxos 🏖️ Naxos · 4 nights (one base!) ⛵ Sailing day ⛵ Paros · optional day trip 🏛️ Athens · 2 nights ✈️ Fly home · Jul 12
Getting around: Athens = metro + walking · Naxos = local bus + boats. You unpack just twice (Naxos, then Athens) — everything else is a day trip. Zero driving.

📅Day by Day

Day 1 · Sun Jul 6

Arrive → Naxos

  • 2:30pm Land at Athens (ATH) ✅ confirmed.
  • 3:30pm Through passport & bags → head to Piraeus port. Taxi ~50 min ~€45 (fastest with luggage) · Metro ~55 min ~€9 · 40 km
  • 4:30pm At Piraeus — quick bite, board.
  • ~5:30pm Last afternoon fast ferry Piraeus → Naxos. ~3h15 (SeaJets) · ~100 nm
  • ~8:45pm Arrive Naxos → hotel. Chora 5-min walk · Agios Prokopios ~10 min taxi, 4 km
  • 9:15pm 🍽️ Dinner: Kastro Naxian Cuisine — gruyère saganaki. Old Town, walkable
Tight but doable. Book a Piraeus→Naxos ferry leaving ~5:00pm or later and take a taxi (faster than the metro with bags). Save the Portara sunset for tomorrow evening. If the flight slips late, grab the first morning ferry Jul 7 and overnight near the airport instead.
Day 2 · Mon Jul 7

⛵ Sailing Day

  • 9:30am To Agia Anna marina. Beach hotels ~5 min · Chora ~10 min taxi, 6 km
  • 10:00am Board a small-group sailboat (intimate, usually ≤10–12 guests) toward the Small Cyclades / Koufonisia.
  • 11:30am Swim & snorkel stops in turquoise bays.
  • 1:00pm 🍽️ Lunch onboard — Greek spread & drinks included on most trips.
  • 5:30pm Sunset sail back to Naxos.
  • 9:00pm 🍽️ Dinner: To Elliniko (Old Town) — local cheeses, slow-cooked lamb.
⛵ Two ways to do it: a small-group full-day sail (~€90–150 pp — best value, still relaxed) or a half-day private charter just for you two (~€500–800). Both include swimming & lunch.
Day 3 · Tue Jul 8

Naxos Beach Day

  • 9:30am Slow breakfast at the hotel.
  • 10:30am Bus to the beach strip (~€2). Agios Prokopios 10 min · Agia Anna 12 min · Plaka 18 min — ~5 km of sand
  • 11am–3pm Swim & sunbathe — soft sand, shallow turquoise water.
  • 1:30pm 🍽️ Lunch: beach taverna (Gorgona, Agia Anna).
  • 6:00pm Sunset drinks back in Chora.
  • 9:00pm 🍽️ Dinner: Axiotissa — farm-to-table. ~20 min taxi, 12 km (worth it)
Day 4 · Wed Jul 9

⛵ Paros Day Trip

  • 9:00am Fast ferry Naxos → Paros (Parikia). ~30–45 min
  • 9:45am Parikia → Naoussa. Bus/taxi ~20 min, 10 km
  • 10:30am Wander Naoussa's Venetian harbor & whitewashed lanes.
  • 12:00pm Water-taxi to Kolymbithres beach — sculpted rock coves. ~10 min across the bay
  • 2:00pm 🍽️ Lunch: Siparos or Sigi Ikthios (fresh seafood).
  • 5:30pm Evening ferry back to Naxos (sleep in Naxos — no packing).
  • 9:00pm 🍽️ Dinner: Dal Professore — fresh pasta at the harbor.
Day 5 · Thu Jul 10

Ferry to Athens

  • 11:00am Fast ferry Naxos → Piraeus. ~3h15
  • 2:30pm Piraeus → Plaka hotel. Metro/taxi ~30–40 min, 12 km
  • 4:00pm Check in; stroll Plaka & Anafiotika (the island-like village under the Acropolis).
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Dinner: rooftop with Acropolis views — Couleur Locale or 360°.
Day 6 · Fri Jul 11

Athens Highlights

  • 8:00am Walk up to the Acropolis early. ~10–15 min uphill from Plaka
  • 10:00am Acropolis Museum. 5-min walk from the exit
  • 1:00pm 🍽️ Lunch: Saita (Plaka) — bakaliaros skordalia.
  • 2:30pm Monastiraki flea market. 10-min walk
  • 7:30pm Sunset from Filopappou Hill (20-min walk) or Lycabettus (taxi/funicular).
  • 9:00pm 🍽️ Last dinner: Akra; nightcap at Materia Prima.
🌅 Optional swap: a Cape Sounion sunset (Temple of Poseidon) — ~70 km, ~1h30 each way by tour or KTEL bus.
Day 7 · Sat Jul 12

Fly Home ✈️ — ATH→EWR 12:45pm

  • 8:30am Leave Plaka. Metro Line 3 (blue) direct to airport, ~40–45 min, ~€9
  • 9:45am At ATH — 3 hrs before an international flight.
  • 12:45pm ✈️ Flight ATH → EWR. No flight-day ferry — that's the whole point of ending in Athens. 🎉

🍽️The Dining Plan

Built for foodies — what to eat each night, where, and what to order. Restaurant picks from the Find Us Lost Greece guides.

🏖️ Naxos (your base — 4 nights)

Day 1 · Dinner (arrival)
Kastro Naxian Cuisine ★
Order: saganaki made with melted Naxian gruyère, on the terrace below the Kastro walls overlooking the harbor.
Day 2 · Lunch (private sail)
Onboard your charter
Order: most private charters include a catered lunch — Greek mezze, fresh fruit, wine. Ask for a seafood spread when booking.
Day 2 · Dinner
To Elliniko (Old Town)
Order: casual but excellent — local cheeses, stuffed vegetables, slow-cooked lamb.
Day 3 · Dinner
Axiotissa ★ (taxi out)
Order: farm-to-table, a Find Us Lost favorite and the standout Naxos meal — seasonal veg, fresh fish, Naxian potatoes.
Day 4 · Dinner
Dal Professore ★ (Chora harbor)
Order: fresh handmade pasta with a harbor sunset. (If you did the Paros day trip, lunch there at Siparos or Sigi Ikthios in Naoussa.)
What to eat in Naxos: famous Naxian potatoes, graviera & arseniko cheeses, and kitron (local citron liqueur) as a digestif.

🏛️ Athens (2 nights)

Day 5 · Dinner (arrival)
Rooftop with Acropolis view — Couleur Locale or 360°
Order: a few mezze, grilled octopus, Greek salad, a glass of Assyrtiko.
Day 6 · Lunch
Saita ★ (Plaka)
Order: their signature bakaliaros skordalia (battered salt cod + fierce garlic dip) — a rare authentic Plaka taverna.
Day 6 · Dinner (last night)
Akra ★
Order: let the rotating organic daily menu lead. After, wine at Materia Prima (pick your own bottle) or dessert at Kokkion (artisanal gelato).
What to eat in Athens: a proper souvlaki/gyro for a cheap lunch and loukoumades (honey doughnuts) for dessert. Alt dinner: Taverna ton Filon.

📞 Reserve ahead (peak July)

  • Naxos Day 1 — Kastro Naxian Cuisine dinner
  • Naxos Day 3 — Axiotissa — books out, reserve early
  • Naxos Day 4 — Dal Professore dinner
  • Athens Day 6 — Akra last-night dinner

💡Quick Tips

  • Book now: July is peak — ferries, Naxos hotels, and boat trips sell out first.
  • Water shoes for rocky swim coves; reef-safe sunscreen.
  • Cash for tavernas, beach bars & small boat operators.
  • Naxos buses run often along the beach strip; grab a taxi for remote tavernas.
  • Build buffer on travel days — ferries can run 15–30 min late.
  • Athens airport metro to the center is ~€9 and easy.
