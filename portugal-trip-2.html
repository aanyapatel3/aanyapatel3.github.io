📅Day by Day

Day 1

🏖️ Arrive Faro → Algarve

  • Midday Land at Faro (FAO), pick up a rental car. ~45–55 min to Lagos / Carvoeiro / Portimão
  • Afternoon Check in & settle into the resort — pool, spa, decompress after the flight.
  • 5:00pm First beach: Praia Dona Ana or Praia da Marinha — golden cliffs & turquoise water.
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Dinner: Vista (your #1 Algarve Beli, at Bela Vista) or grilled fish on the Portimão marina.
🚗 A rental car is worth it in the Algarve — the beaches & best tables are spread along the coast and not walkable. Keep it just for these two days and return it at Faro before the Lisbon train; you'll go car-free (trains + Uber/Bolt) for the rest of the trip.
Day 2

⛵ Benagil Caves & Cliffs

  • 9:30am Boat tour to the Benagil Sea Cave — the famous domed cave with the sky-hole, plus hidden grottoes (kayak/SUP tours can land inside).
  • 12:00pm Swim & sunbathe at Praia da Marinha (one of Europe's most beautiful beaches).
  • 2:00pm 🍽️ Lunch: cliffside seafood in Carvoeiro or at Restaurante F (Praia da Rocha, your Beli).
  • 4:30pm Ponta da Piedade (Lagos) — ochre rock arches & the cliff boardwalk; sunset drinks.
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Dinner: Vila Joya (2★) or Bon Bon (1★) — the Algarve's destination tables.
⛵ Do Benagil at 9–10am to beat the midday boat traffic & actually get inside the cave.
Day 3

Algarve → Lisbon

  • Morning Return the car at Faro, then train Faro → Lisboa (~3h) — or a quick flight. Comfortable intercity train, scenic
  • Afternoon Check in, then wander Chiado & Príncipe Real — a first bifana at O Trevo & a Manteigaria nata.
  • 6:30pm Sunset at Miradouro de São Pedro de Alcântara with a glass of white port.
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Dinner: Belcanto (2★) or Cervejaria Ramiro — then a nightcap at Boavista Social Club.
Day 4

Lisbon — Alfama & Belém

  • 9:00am Alfama — ride Tram 28, climb to Castelo de São Jorge for the river view.
  • 12:00pm 🍽️ Lunch: chicken at A Valenciana — or piri-piri takeaway from Frangasqueira Nacional.
  • 1:30pm Belém — Jerónimos Monastery & Belém Tower, warm Pastéis de Belém + a bake at Maruoko.
  • 4:00pm MAAT riverfront + LX Factory (design shops & street art).
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Dinner: Alma (2★) — or casual at Karater (Georgian) / Taberna Carolina.
🏰 Love Sintra? With only two Lisbon days it's a tight squeeze — swap this city day for a Sintra day trip (Pena Palace + Quinta da Regaleira) if the palaces matter more than Belém. Option 1 builds Sintra in fully.
Day 5

🍷 Lisbon → Douro Valley

  • 9:00am Alfa Pendular train Lisboa → Porto (~2h50), then a scenic transfer to the Douro Valley. ~1h30 more
  • 2:30pm Check in at Six Senses Douro Valley — terrace lunch, vineyard infinity pool, a glass of white port.
  • 4:30pm Guided tasting in the Wine Library + an estate stroll.
  • 8:00pm 🍽️ Dinner at the resort (Vale de Abraão), relaxed after the travel day.
Day 6

🍇 Douro Wine Day — Pinhão

  • 10:00am 🚂 Scenic train Régua → Pinhão — one of the world's prettiest rail rides (sit river side).
  • 10:30am Explore Pinhão & its azulejo-tiled train station; a private driver meets you here.
  • 11:15am Quinta tour & tasting — Quinta do Bomfim (Dow's) or Quinta Nova.
  • 1:00pm 🍽️ Vineyard lunch with wine pairing — Quinta de la Rosa or Quinta do Bomfim.
  • 3:30pm ⛵ Rabelo boat tour from Pinhão + the Casal de Loivos viewpoint.
  • 8:00pm 🍽️ Dinner: Six Senses sommelier pairings, or DOC by Rui Paula on the river.

📖 Douro experiences — what to expect

Tap any one to expand. Six Senses can arrange all of these.

🚂 Scenic train · Régua → Pinhão

A ~25-min ride hugging the river through the steepest terraces — one of the world's prettiest. Sit on the river (right) side. A vintage steam train (Régua–Tua) runs select summer Saturdays.

🏘️ Pinhão & the azulejo station

Tiny riverside town at the heart of port country; its 1937 station is lined with blue-and-white tiles of the harvest. ~30–45 min, then your hub for the quintas & boat.

🛥️ Rabelo / vintage-boat cruise

A 1-hour cruise from Pinhão past the terraces in the traditional flat-bottomed boats. For top-end, Pipadouro runs restored classic wooden boats. Afternoon light is best.

🧺 Picnic + foot-treading

Several quintas set up a vineyard picnic; Quinta do Tedo is known for picnics, organic-port tours, and — in the September harvest — grape foot-treading.

🍇 Quinta tastings & viewpoints

Tastings (1–1.5h) at Quinta do Crasto (infinity-pool view, by appt), Quinta do Bomfim, Quinta Nova, Quinta de Nápoles. Best viewpoints: São Leonardo de Galafura, Casal de Loivos, São Salvador do Mundo.

Day 7

Douro → Porto

  • 10:30am Slow morning at the resort, then transfer to Porto. ~1h30
  • 1:00pm Check in, then 🍽️ lunch in the Ribeira — Taberna dos Mercadores (your Beli) by the Dom Luís I Bridge.
  • 4:00pm Livraria Lello & São Bento station's azulejo hall.
  • 6:00pm Cross to Vila Nova de Gaia for a port cellar tasting (Graham's / Taylor's) at golden hour.
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Dinner: Cozinha das Flores or Euskalduna Studio (your Beli).
Day 8

Porto at Leisure

  • 9:30am Brunch (Zenith / do Norte), then the Bolsa Palace & Clérigos Tower.
  • 12:30pm 🍽️ Lunch: the famous francesinha at Café Santiago — or your Beli: Gruta / MISTU.
  • 2:30pm Tram to Foz do Douro — where the river meets the Atlantic; oceanfront promenade.
  • 5:00pm Cedofeita design shops & galleries (peek at Apego / Atrevo).
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Dinner: The Yeatman (2★) or Pedro Lemos (1★) in Foz.
Day 9

Porto — Bonus Day

  • Morning Your call: the Serralves museum & gardens, more Gaia cellars, or a half-day trip to Guimarães (Portugal's birthplace) or Braga. ~45–60 min by train
  • 1:00pm 🍽️ Lunch out — a Beli spot like Pata Gorda or InDiferente.
  • 4:00pm A Douro river cruise from the Ribeira (the Six Bridges loop), or last shopping.
  • 8:30pm 🍽️ Last dinner: Le Monument or Antiqvvm (your Beli).
🗺️ The 3rd Porto night gives you a flexible bonus day — pick a day trip, a museum, or just a slow Porto day. Save the easiest plan for last so the flight day is stress-free.
Day 10

Fly Home ✈️ — from Porto

  • Morning Last pastel de nata, then to Porto Airport (OPO). ~25–30 min by Uber/Bolt or metro
  • Midday ✈️ Fly home from Porto. 🎉

💡Quick Tips

  • Open-jaw flight: in Faro (FAO), out Porto (OPO) — no backtracking.
  • Rent the car only for the Algarve; return it at Faro and go car-free (trains + Uber/Bolt) after.
  • Benagil at 9–10am beats the crowds; book the cave boat ahead.
  • Two Lisbon days is tight — decide between a city day (Belém) and a Sintra day trip.
  • Book the marquee tables & Six Senses early (Belcanto, Alma, Vila Joya, The Yeatman).
  • Shoulder season (May/Jun, Sep/Oct) is ideal — warm coast, gorgeous Douro, fewer crowds.
